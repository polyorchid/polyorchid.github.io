/// OPERATOR BRIEFING

Core loop

Run probes with Space, Enter, or the big probe button to earn cash. Buy items in the Hack Shop for passive IPS (income per second). Pick Targets to raise your payout multiplier — higher targets unlock as your balance grows.

Prestige & fragments

When you have at least $1,000, use [ PRESTIGE ] to wipe money, items, and targets in exchange for FRAG (Fragments). More money at prestige = more FRAG. After your first prestige, [ FRAG SHOP ] opens for permanent buffs, themes, and utilities.

Run tier vs prestige count

  • RUN tier gates which shop items and targets you can access. It advances from lifetime FRAG earned, not how many times you've prestiged.
  • PRST is simply how many times you've prestiged.
  • Spending FRAG in the shop does not lower your run tier.

Saves & tools

  • Progress autosaves every 5 minutes and when you tab away. Use [ SAVE ] to force a save.
  • [ RESET ] permanently deletes all progress.
  • The console accepts commands — try help. Use [ POP OUT ] for a larger terminal.
  • [ SCI: OFF/ON ] toggles scientific notation for huge numbers.
